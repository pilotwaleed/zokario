Zokario is a small independent house. If the rooms here have given you something, you can give a little back: any amount, once, whenever you like. Patrons carry a candle by their name, forever.

Contributions are voluntary and non-refundable; they buy nothing but our gratitude and a candle symbol by your name. Demo mode: no real charge.
